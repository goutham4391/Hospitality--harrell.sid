    Why Harrell?

    It’s our Breadth of Experience!

    Our Executives have experience in:

    • Management of hotels from limited service to select service, to full service to luxury, located in primary, secondary and tertiary cities in the USA, Mexico and the Caribbean, comprising more than 100 hotels and 26,000 hotel rooms.
    • Development of hotels from the ground up, from financial modeling to FF&E purchasing, to preopening marketing, staffing and training, all the way through to the opening of the hotels
